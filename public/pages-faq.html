Gym rules

1. Memberships are personal. It is not allowed to lend your chip to others. Always bring your chip when using the gym.

2. Smoking and consumption of alcohol are strictly prohibited.

3. Machines must be handled properly. This especially refers to the weight restrictions of the machines.

4. It is the responsibility of each member to clean up after themselves.

5. Machines must not be moved from their place. After use, weights must be put back in place.

6. The gym's board can, at any time and without notice, close the gym, for example during holidays, public holidays, and cleaning, etc.

7. The use of the gym's equipment is at your own risk. Damage to equipment will result in liability for replacement.

8. Membership of the gym will be automatically terminated due to non-payment.

9. Violation of these rules may result in immediate termination of membership. In special cases, the gym's board may decide whether further exclusion should take place.
